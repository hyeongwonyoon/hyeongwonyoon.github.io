School Life at Handong

  • Team Meeting
    • Each member of HGU will be placed in a team with 30 other students, having a team professor who will caring and advising you for a year. Being in a team will help you make friends and do lot of fun activities together. What to learn more about international teams? Visit Carmichael Residential College Facebook page Facebook
  • Festival & Contest
    • HGU provides a Global Culture Festival once a year, where many different cultures are represented. Come and try the food and learn about different culture.
  • Handong International Church (HIC)
    • HIC serves a multi-ethnic congregation comprised of Handong members. HIC is made up of opportunities given throughout the year such as: worship services in English, Morning Prayer, English Discipleship, School and Life Groups. Find out more about HIC at church.handong.edu
  • Activities
    • There are many other experiences and events within HGU. Amongst them are the International Buddy System(IBS); to befriend a Korean student with many opportunities to spend a fun time.
